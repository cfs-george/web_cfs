Effective Date: 11th November 2025

Cyberfreak Software Limited (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information collected through the CFS React Native Boilerplate app (“App”).

The App only collects the following information:

No other personal information, location data, or usage data is collected.

The email addresses you provide are only used:

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.
